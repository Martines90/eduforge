## üéØ SCENARIO SELECTION STRATEGY

### Step 1: Identify Literary Analytical Skill
- Character analysis ‚Üí Who needs to understand characters? (Directors, actors, screenwriters)
- Symbolism ‚Üí Who interprets symbols? (Scholars, translators, curators)
- Theme ‚Üí Who identifies themes? (Publishers, editors, curriculum designers)
- Technique ‚Üí Who analyzes craft? (Translators, writing teachers, poets)
- Context ‚Üí Who needs historical understanding? (Dramaturgs, museums, legal scholars)

### Step 2: Find Authentic Professional Application
Ask: "What professionals actually do this type of literary analysis for real decisions/creations?"

### Step 3: Choose Specific, High-Stakes Context
- Avoid generic "analyze this text"
- Choose specific professional moment with real consequences
- Make the stakes clear (money, truth, artistic success, justice, etc.)

### Step 4: Verify Text-Analysis Fit
- Would this professional actually read the text closely?
- Would they analyze the specific element you're asking about?
- Is the literary analysis the KEY to solving their problem?
